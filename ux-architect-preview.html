Required Scanner Language Template

Research Band

Build Your Research Band

Choose the research filters the scanner uses to show matching Iron Condor candidates. This is not building a trade.

Run

Run Scanner

Show candidates that match the selected research band. Avoid “best trades,” “signals,” or recommendation language.

Pagination

Load Next 50 Candidates

Loads the next group of matching candidates so the page stays fast, organized, and readable.

Board

Scanner Board

The candidate review board. Each row is one educational candidate structure, not a recommendation list.

Ticket

Research Ticket

Organizes one candidate’s details for educational review. It is not a broker order ticket and does not place a trade.

Readout

Scanner Readout

Use “Scanner Readout” or “Review Status.” Do not use “Signal” as a public-facing label.

UX Architect Working Brief

This page is the template location for the UX Architect’s proposed site experience. The live homepage remains unchanged while this draft evolves.

  • Keep the scanner on its own dedicated Live Scanner Lab page.
  • Promote the Academy from the homepage.

  • Keep all language educational, compliance-conscious, and user-friendly.
