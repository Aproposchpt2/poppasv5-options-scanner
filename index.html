What the columns mean
DTE — Calendar days to expiration · the monthly expiry date.
NED — Next Earnings Date (within 90 days). Red ⚠ = lands before expiry (condor shielded); "—" = none within 90 days.
IV — How pricey insurance is right now.
Expected Move — Approximate IV-implied move by expiration: spot × IV × √(DTE/365). This is an estimate, not a prediction or guarantee.
EM Status — Compares the short strikes with the expected-move range: Outside EM, Near EM, or Inside EM.
IV Status — Premium richness from IV: Very Deflated/Deflated (cheap) → Fair → Inflated/Very Inflated (rich = fatter credit to sell).
Mo. OI — Combined open interest of the selected monthly expiration. The default review floor is ≥ 10,000.
Short OI — Lower open interest of the selected short put/short call anchors. This is separate from monthly OI.
Anchor P(OTM) — Measured on the short put and short call only. The table shows the lower/binding anchor probability; this is not a guaranteed whole-condor probability.
ROC — Conservative net credit divided by max risk.
All-Leg B/A — Widest bid/ask gap across all four legs where available; the default review floor is ≤ $0.05.
